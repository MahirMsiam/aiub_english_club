Rules & Regulations

Guidelines and policies of AIUB English Club

Club Membership Rules

Eligibility

  • Must be a current student of American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB)
  • Must maintain a minimum CGPA as set by the university
  • Must commit to regular participation in club activities
  • Must pay the annual membership fee (if applicable)

Conduct & Behavior

  • Members must maintain professional and respectful behavior at all times
  • Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind is strictly prohibited
  • All members must follow AIUB's code of conduct
  • Active listening and participation are encouraged in all activities
  • Members must respect others' opinions and viewpoints

Attendance Policy

  • Members are expected to attend at least 70% of scheduled meetings and events
  • Advance notice should be given if unable to attend an event
  • Repeated absences may result in membership suspension
  • Officers must maintain 100% attendance at executive meetings

Responsibilities

  • Members must complete assigned tasks on time
  • Officers must lead by example and fulfill their designated roles
  • Members must communicate any issues or concerns to club leadership
  • All members are responsible for maintaining club standards and reputation

Event Participation

  • Members must comply with event-specific guidelines
  • Photography/video recording is allowed only with permission
  • Content shared on social media must be appropriate and authorized
  • Members are responsible for setting up and cleaning after events

Financial Policies

  • All financial transactions must be transparent and recorded
  • Club funds should be used only for authorized club activities
  • Budget proposals must be approved by the executive committee
  • Financial records will be audited regularly

Disciplinary Action

  • Violations of rules may result in warnings or suspension
  • Serious violations may lead to permanent expulsion
  • Members have the right to appeal disciplinary decisions
  • The executive committee will review all cases fairly

Important Notes

These rules are designed to ensure a positive and productive environment for all members. All members are expected to familiarize themselves with these guidelines and comply with them. The executive committee reserves the right to modify these rules as needed, with proper notification to all members.
