Vision

Our vision is to create a community of English enthusiasts who are passionate about language and culture.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to enhance their English language skills through various activities and events. We aim to foster a love for the English language and promote cultural exchange among students.

About Us

The AIUB English Club at American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB) is a vibrant student-led community dedicated to promoting English and fostering confident communication. We aim to develop students into effective speakers, critical thinkers, and dynamic leaders. Our club offers an inclusive space to explore English beyond textbooks through activities like public speaking, creative writing, debates, and storytelling. We host interactive workshops, competitions, cultural events, language games, and professional development sessions to sharpen communication skills. We believe language is about expression, connection, and confidence. Here, students don't just learn English; they live it. Our mission is to empower students through meaningful communication, critical thinking, and leadership experiences. We believe every voice matters. In a globally connected world, strong English communication is vital, and we are preparing future changemakers. Whether you want to polish speaking skills, gain confidence, or make new friends, the AIUB English Club welcomes you. Come speak with purpose. Grow with us and make every word matter.
